The US-China Tariff War: An Overview

A comprehensive look at the ongoing trade dispute between the United States and China, summarizing key insights from leading news sources.

Introduction

The US-China tariff war is a complex and multifaceted trade dispute that has significant global implications. This website provides a summary of key news articles to help understand the various aspects of this conflict.

Key Articles Summaries

A test of endurance: Who will blink first in the US-China tariff war? (ThinkChina)

This article discusses the impact of tariffs on China, examining the endurance of both sides in the trade war and questioning who will concede first.

Trump tariff nightmare continues to plague markets as US dollar plunges on trade war, recession fears (The Straits Times)

The article summarizes market reactions and recession fears resulting from the tariff war, including the plunge of the US dollar.

US-China tariff war could cut trade between the two by 80%: WTO chief (The Business Times)

This piece highlights the WTO chief's warning about the potential impact of the tariff war, projecting a possible 80% reduction in trade between the US and China.

China is not backing down from Trump's tariff war. What next? (BBC)

The BBC article analyzes China's firm stance in the tariff war and explores potential future developments.

'Bring it on, Trump, we're ready': Chinese stand firm on tariff war despite risks (South China Morning Post)

This article reports on Chinese public opinion, indicating a firm stance despite the risks associated with the tariff war.

Trump’s tariff war: What’s at stake for China’s economy? (Al Jazeera)

Al Jazeera's analysis focuses on the economic risks for China resulting from Trump's tariff war.

Trump tariff war puts UK financial stability at risk, Bank of England warns (POLITICO.eu)

The article summarizes the Bank of England's warning about the risks to UK financial stability due to the tariff war.

China says Trump’s trade war ‘will end in failure’ as Beijing tariffs take effect (The Guardian)

This article reports on China's response, stating that the trade war will end in failure, and the implementation of Beijing's tariffs.

How China’s record trade surplus helped spark Trump’s tariff war (Financial Times)

The Financial Times explains how China's record trade surplus contributed to the initiation of Trump's tariff war.

China hits back at Trump tariff hike, raises duties on US goods (Reuters)

Reuters reports on China's retaliatory measures, including raising duties on US goods in response to Trump's tariff hike.
